## 🚀 Quick Start (5 Minutes)

### What Is This?

An AI-powered productivity system that helps you create **world-class outputs** for any knowledge work:
- Reports and dashboards
- Presentations and decks
- Data analysis and insights
- Creative content (ads, push notifications, banners)
- Documentation and onboarding materials
- Process improvements and playbooks

**You don't need to know AI.** Just describe what you need in plain language.

### Your First Task (2 Minutes)

1. **Open Cursor**
2. **Type your request** in plain language:

```
Help me create a weekly performance report for my restaurant portfolio.
I have the data in this CSV file.
```

3. **Answer a few questions** (the system will ask what it needs)
4. **Get your output** — professionally formatted, ready to use

### That's It!

The system figures out which tools to use, asks clarifying questions, and delivers quality outputs.

## 🔍 How It Works (Optional Reading)

### Behind the Scenes

When you make a request:

```
Your Request
    ↓
🤖 Orchestrator (understands what you need)
    ↓
📋 Selects the right specialists
    ↓
❓ Asks clarifying questions (if needed)
    ↓
🔨 Creates your output
    ↓
✅ Quality check
    ↓
📦 Delivers to you
```

### Do This ✅

|-----|---------|

| **Mention the output format** | "...as a presentation" or "...as a one-pager" |

| Don't | Do Instead |
|-------|------------|
| "Make something" | "Make a presentation about X for Y audience" |
| "Analyze everything" | "Find the top 3 insights from this data" |
| "Be creative" | "Create 3 options for a push notification about X" |

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Do I need to know which agent to use?**  
A: No. Just describe your task. The system picks the right tools.

**Q: What if the output isn't perfect?**  
A: Give feedback! Say "make it shorter" or "add more detail" or "change the colors." The system learns your preferences.

**Q: Can it access our company data?**  
A: It can read files you provide (CSV, Excel, etc.) and any context files in the `/Context` folder.

**Q: What if it can't do something?**  
A: It will tell you! And suggest whether a new capability should be added.
