## Reflection Prompts Library

### When Feeling Stuck
- What would I do if I knew I couldn't fail?
- What am I pretending not to know?
- What would I advise a friend in this situation?
- What's the smallest possible next step?

### When Feeling Overwhelmed
- What can I control right now?
- What's actually urgent vs. just noisy?
- What can I let go of or delegate?
- What would "good enough" look like?

### When Making Decisions
- What will I regret NOT doing?
- What does my gut say (before my brain talks me out of it)?
- What would the person I want to become choose?
- What's the worst that could happen, and can I handle it?

### When Celebrating Wins
- What did I do that made this possible?
- Who helped me get here?
- What skills did I develop?
- How can I build on this momentum?

### When Processing Failure
- What actually happened vs. the story I'm telling myself?
- What can I learn from this?
- How will this make me better?
- What's the gift in this challenge?
