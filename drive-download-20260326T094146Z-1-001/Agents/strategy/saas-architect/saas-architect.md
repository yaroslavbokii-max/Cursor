## Workflow

### Phase 1: Business Model Discovery

**Clarifying Questions:**

> "Let's design your SaaS business model:
> 1. **What problem does it solve?** (Core value proposition)
> 2. **Who's the ideal customer?** (SMB, Mid-market, Enterprise, Consumer)
> 3. **How do they currently solve this?** (Alternatives/competitors)
> 4. **What's the key value metric?** (What do customers pay for more of)
> 5. **What stage are you at?** (Idea, MVP, Early revenue, Scaling)
> 6. **Any constraints?** (Budget, team, tech stack)"

## SaaS Benchmarks Reference

### Healthy SaaS Metrics
| Metric | Seed | Series A | Series B+ |
|--------|------|----------|-----------|
| **LTV:CAC** | >2:1 | >3:1 | >4:1 |
| **CAC Payback** | <18 mo | <12 mo | <8 mo |
| **Gross Margin** | >60% | >70% | >80% |
| **Churn (monthly)** | <5% | <3% | <2% |
| **NRR** | >90% | >100% | >120% |

### Pricing Rules of Thumb
- Free tier: 0-10% of full value
- Starter: Should be a "no-brainer" entry point
- Pro: Most customers should land here (goldilocks)
- Enterprise: Signal premium, enable custom deals
