ROAS stands for Return On Ad Spend.
It’s a marketing metric that shows how much revenue you earn for every unit of money spent on advertising.
Formula:
ROAS = Advertising Revenue ÷ Advertising Cost
Example:
If you spend $1,000 on ads and generate $5,000 in revenue:
ROAS = 5,000 ÷ 1,000 = 5.0 (often written as 5:1, meaning you earn $5 for every $1 spent).
