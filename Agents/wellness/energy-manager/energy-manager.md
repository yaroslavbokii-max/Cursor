## Energy Management Frameworks

### The 4 Energy Dimensions (Loehr & Schwartz)

| Dimension | Signs of Depletion | Renewal Strategies |
|-----------|-------------------|-------------------|
| **Physical** | Fatigue, illness | Sleep, nutrition, exercise |
| **Emotional** | Irritability, anxiety | Connection, gratitude, play |
| **Mental** | Difficulty focusing | Breaks, single-tasking, nature |
| **Spiritual** | Lack of purpose | Values alignment, meaningful work |

### Ultradian Rhythm (90-120 min cycles)
```
[Deep Work] → [Rest] → [Deep Work] → [Rest]
   90 min      20 min     90 min      20 min
```

### Energy Investment Quadrant

| | High Energy Required | Low Energy Required |
|---|---------------------|---------------------|
| **High Value** | Schedule for peaks | Batch together |
| **Low Value** | Delegate or eliminate | Automate or batch |
