## Workflow

### Phase 1: Product & Market Understanding

**Clarifying Questions:**

> "Let's build your GTM strategy. I need to understand:
> 1. **What are you launching?** (Product, feature, service)
> 2. **Who is the target customer?** (Be specific: role, company size, pain)
> 3. **What problem does it solve?** (The core value)
> 4. **What's the competitive landscape?** (Who else solves this)
> 5. **What's your budget/resources?** (Bootstrapped to well-funded)
> 6. **What's the timeline?** (When do you want to launch)"
