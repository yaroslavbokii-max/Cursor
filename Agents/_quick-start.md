## 🎯 Three Ways to Use This

### 1. Quick Shot (Fastest)
**For:** Simple, single tasks

```markdown
Just describe what you want:
"Analyze sales data" + [attach file]
```

### 2. Guided Flow (Recommended)
**For:** Most tasks

```markdown
Start with: "Help me with..."
The system will:
1. Understand your goal
2. Ask 1-2 questions if needed
3. Show you the plan
4. Execute
```

### 3. Expert Mode (Power Users)
**For:** When you know exactly what you want

```markdown
Call specific agents directly:
@data-analyst analyze this
@presentation-maker create slides about X
```

## 💡 Tips for Best Results

### Do This ✅

```markdown
Be specific about:
- What you want (output)
- Who it's for (audience)
- Any constraints (length, format, style)

Example:
"Create a 5-slide presentation about Q4 results 
for the executive team, McKinsey style, 
focus on the revenue drop and recovery plan"
```

### Don't Do This ❌

```markdown
Too vague:
"Help me with my presentation"

Missing context:
"Analyze this" (no file attached)

Too many things at once:
"Analyze data, make presentation, write email, and automate it"
```

## 📊 What You'll Get

### Every Output Includes:

1. **The actual deliverable** (analysis, presentation, content)
2. **Confidence score** (how reliable is this)
3. **Key assumptions** (what was assumed)
4. **Next steps** (what to do with this)

### Example Output:

```markdown
## Analysis: Q4 Revenue Drop

**Confidence: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (High)**

### Finding
Revenue dropped 15% due to SMB churn spike after pricing change.

### Key Assumptions
- Churn timing correlates with pricing (not proven causal)

### Recommended Next Steps
1. Win-back campaign for churned SMBs
2. Competitive pricing analysis
3. Consider tiered pricing
```

## ❓ FAQ

### "What if I don't know which agent to use?"
Don't worry about it. Just describe your task. The system picks the right agents.

### "What if my request is unclear?"
The system will ask 1-2 questions to clarify. Just answer them.

### "What if the output isn't what I wanted?"
Say "That's not quite right, I wanted [clarification]" and it'll adjust.

### "Can I see what agents are available?"
Yes: Check `_catalog.md` or ask "What agents do you have for [topic]?"

### "How do I give feedback?"
After any output, you'll see: [👍 Great] [👎 Needs work]
Click one. Your feedback improves future outputs.
